Privacy Policy

PI Video Editor

Last updated: April 7, 2026

PI Video Editor is a macOS app for importing, reviewing, arranging, trimming, and exporting video clips and still images. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

What the app accesses

The app only accesses videos, images, and folders that you explicitly choose. It does not scan your Mac or access files unless you open them in the app.

How your data is processed

Editing, timestamp handling, clip ordering, trimming, and export operations are performed locally on your device. Your media files are not uploaded to my servers for processing.

Internet access

PI Video Editor works offline and does not require an account or internet connection to use its core features.

Data collection

I do not collect personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data through PI Video Editor.

Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Local storage

The app may store local preferences on your device, such as display settings, export options, and other app preferences. This information remains on your device.

Your control

  • You choose which files and folders the app can access.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can remove local app data by deleting the app and its local preferences from your device.
